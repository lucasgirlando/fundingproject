Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") applies to https://miamifundinghub.com, and Miami Funding Hub LLC ("Company") and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to the Company include https://miamifundinghub.com. The Company's website is a lead generation referral service for small and medium-sized businesses seeking working capital from third-party funding providers. By using the Company website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Important Service Clarification

Miami Funding Hub LLC is a referral and consulting service only. We do not provide direct lending services, make funding decisions, or hold custody of client funds. All funding decisions are made by independent third-party providers.

California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") and California Privacy Rights Act ("CPRA"):

  • Right to Know. You may request details on what personal data we collect, use, and share.
  • Right to Delete. You can request deletion of personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Correct. You may request corrections to inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out. You can opt out of the sale or sharing of personal data for advertising.
  • Right to Restrict Sensitive Data Use. You may limit the use of sensitive personal information.
  • Right Against Retaliation. The Company will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Collection of your Personal Information

In order to better provide you with referral services offered, the Company may collect personally identifiable information, such as your:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business Name, Monthly Business Revenue, Years In Business, Industry, Funding Amount Requested, Use of Funds
  • Communication preferences and interaction history

We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us. However, you may be required to provide certain personal information to us when you elect to use certain products or services.

Use of your Personal Information

The Company collects and uses your personal information in the following ways:

  • To operate and deliver the referral services you have requested
  • To connect you with appropriate third-party funding providers
  • To provide you with information about funding opportunities
  • To provide you with notices about your application status
  • To carry out the Company's obligations under our referral agreements
  • To notify you about changes to our services
  • To improve our matching and referral processes
  • For any other purpose with your consent

Data Retention and Timeline

  • Active Period: We retain your information for up to 90 days for active referral purposes
  • Follow-Up Period: We may retain contact information for up to 2 years to provide additional funding opportunities
  • Legal Requirements: Some information may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations
  • Deletion Requests: You may request deletion of your personal information at any time (subject to legal exceptions)

Sharing Information with Third-Party Funding Providers

Important: The primary purpose of our service is to share your information with qualified third-party funding providers to facilitate funding opportunities.

By using our service, you expressly consent to us sharing your personal and business information with:

  • Third-party lending institutions
  • Alternative funding providers
  • Business financing companies
  • Credit assessment services
  • Partner referral services

What We Share: We may share all information you provide including personal details, business information, funding requirements, and financial data necessary for funding evaluation.

Provider Requirements: All funding providers are required to:

  • Maintain confidentiality of your information
  • Use information solely for funding evaluation purposes
  • Comply with applicable privacy and data protection laws
  • Provide their own privacy policies for direct interactions

Other Third-Party Sharing

The Company does not sell, rent, or lease customer lists for marketing purposes unrelated to funding services.

The Company may share data with trusted service providers to help perform statistical analysis, send communications, provide customer support, or facilitate referrals. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to the Company.

The Company may disclose your personal information, without notice, if required to do so by law or in good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to legal requirements; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of the Company; and/or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect user safety.

Funding Decision Communications

We may communicate with you regarding:

  • Status updates on referrals to funding providers
  • Additional funding opportunities that become available
  • General market information and funding tips
  • Provider feedback (when available and appropriate)

Important: We do not make funding decisions and cannot guarantee approval or specific terms from any third-party provider.

Tracking User Behavior

The Company may keep track of website usage to determine which services are most popular and to improve our referral matching process. This data helps us provide better service and more relevant funding opportunities.

Automatically Collected Information

The Company may automatically collect information about your computer hardware and software including IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, and referring website addresses. This information is used for service operation, quality maintenance, and usage statistics.

Links

This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites, including third-party funding provider websites.

Security of your Personal Information

The Company secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure using methods including:

  • SSL Protocol
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Restricted access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments

When personal information is transmitted to third-party funding providers, it is protected through encryption and secure transmission protocols.

Right to Deletion

Subject to certain exceptions, on receipt of a verifiable request from you, we will:

  • Delete your personal information from our records
  • Direct service providers to delete your personal information from their records
  • Notify relevant third-party providers of your deletion request (where applicable)

Please note: We may not be able to comply with deletion requests if information is necessary to:

  • Complete ongoing funding evaluations or transactions
  • Detect security incidents or protect against fraudulent activity
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Exercise free speech rights or comply with other legal requirements

Data Retention for Business Purposes

  • Up to 90 days for active referral purposes
  • Up to 2 years for follow-up funding opportunities (unless you opt out)
  • Longer periods as required by law or for legal protection

Children Under Thirteen
